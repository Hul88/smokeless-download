> **Track. Awareness. Control.**

Smokeless is a minimalistic, privacy-focused Android application designed to help you reduce or quit smoking through awareness and subtle gamification.

## ‚ú® Features
*   **Privacy First**: All data is stored locally on your device. No cloud, no tracking.
*   **Expense & Habit Tracking**: Track cigarettes smoked and money spent in real-time.
*   **Dynamic UI**: The "SMOKE" button **physically shrinks** for every day you stay smoke-free.
*   **Themes**: Automatic Light/Dark mode support (System Sync).
*   **Stats**: View your 7-day average, total life lost, and money saved.
*   **Mental Reinforcement**:
    *   **Encouragement**: Motivational quotes on the home screen.
    *   **Disappointment**: Subtle "guilt" popups when you reset your streak.
